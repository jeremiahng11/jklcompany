Komei Foong

Multi-talented Singer, Actress, and Emcee

About Komei Foong

Komei Foong began her journey in the world of music at the tender age of 7, captivating audiences on the vibrant getai stage—a traditional form of entertainment during the Hungry Ghost Festival in Malaysia and Singapore. Her early debut showcased her natural talent and passion for singing, which would become a defining aspect of her life.

Musical Style

Komei is renowned for her sweet and melodic voice, often drawing comparisons to the legendary Teresa Teng—a beloved icon known for her romantic ballads that have transcended generations. Komei’s vocal prowess, combined with her emotional delivery, has earned her a special place in the hearts of fans across the region.

Emcee Experience

With over a decade of experience as a master of ceremonies (MC), Komei has hosted numerous high-profile events. Her engaging personality and professionalism have made her a sought-after Emcee for private functions, government events, and charity gatherings, where she brings her unique charm and flair to every occasion.

Livestreams & Live Selling

Komei's online livestreaming journey began during the early days of COVID-19. She has worked with various businesses, introducing and selling products alongside co-host celebrities on platforms such as Facebook and TikTok. Her engaging presence helps boost brand exposure and sales.

Acting Experience

With a decade of on-set experience at MediaCorp, Komei has played small roles in Jack Neo’s comedy TV series and movies, as well as numerous commercials with major brands. She was a main cast member in the highly anticipated 2025 CNY movie "I Want to Be Boss" directed by Jack Neo, and she will feature in the 2026 movie by Eric Wong, showcasing her acting and musical talents in Singapore.

Sponsored Content

Komei offers quality content creation featuring herself, providing sponsors with impressive reach and exposure through her social media channels. She has long-standing partnerships with brands in postpartum health, Malay massages, beauty slimming packages, and more, engaging her dedicated fan base with authentic content.

Continuing the Passion

Komei Foong continues to inspire many with her dedication to her craft. As a talented singer, actress, and emcee, she remains a beloved figure in the entertainment scenes of Malaysia and Singapore, constantly evolving and connecting with her audience through her versatile talents.
